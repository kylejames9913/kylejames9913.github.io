WHO WE ARE

TEGRITY COMPANY is a coalition of outdoorsmen who intend to uphold Integrity

as individuals and of the resources that they utilize to pursue their passions in.

Our products are reflective of our experiences spent outdoors

and are brought to you in the highest of quality, built to last and stay tough.

We aim to capture the ruggedness and grit of the West along with a deep

Respect for her many rivers and peaks.
